Each of the GSPs’ ten (10) Doctoral programmes and six (6) Master’s programmes is autonomous and based at one lead university selected in a consultative process among the candidates’ countries. The GSPs pool the scientific and educational strength of faculties in the West African region and thereby facilitate high quality research and education.
